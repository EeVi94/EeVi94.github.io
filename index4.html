The Draft and Final 3d Prints from my LeagueLogoMC (Look at MineCraft Sculpture)

In e-sports culture, League of Legends, has made a huge impact in internet gaming community and being able to gain sponsors and enough money to create organizations that compete competitively in multiple splits. The size difference I created here from my first draft to my second, correlates to the amount of which it exploded in popularity in the online gaming community and how it only continues to grow!
